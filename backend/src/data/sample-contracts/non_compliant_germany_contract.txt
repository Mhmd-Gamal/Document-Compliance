ARBEITSVERTRAG (EMPLOYMENT CONTRACT)

Between:
GlobalTech GmbH, Friedrichstraße 123, 10117 Berlin, Germany ("Arbeitgeber/Employer")

And:
Maria Schmidt, residing at Hauptstraße 45, 10115 Berlin ("Arbeitnehmer/Employee")

Effective Date: February 1, 2024

§1 POSITION AND DUTIES
Employee is employed as Product Manager. Duties include product strategy, roadmap management, and cross-functional team coordination.

§2 TERM OF EMPLOYMENT
This is a PERMANENT employment contract (unbefristeter Arbeitsvertrag).

§3 PROBATIONARY PERIOD
The first 6 months constitute a probationary period during which either party may terminate with 2 weeks notice.

§4 COMPENSATION
Gross monthly salary: €5,500 (sixty-six thousand euros annually)
Payment: Monthly, by the last day of each month.
Note: This exceeds the statutory minimum wage.

§5 WORKING HOURS
Regular working hours: 40 hours per week, Monday through Friday.
Core hours: 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM with flexible start/end times.
Overtime: All overtime must be pre-approved. Overtime up to 10 hours monthly is included in salary.

§6 ANNUAL LEAVE
*** VIOLATION: Employee is entitled to 10 working days of paid vacation per calendar year. ***
[Note: This violates BUrlG which mandates minimum 20 days for 5-day week]

§7 SICK LEAVE
In case of illness, Employee must notify Employer immediately on the first day of absence.
*** VIOLATION: Medical certificate required from DAY 1 of illness. ***
Employee receives continued salary payment for first 6 weeks as per law.

§8 TERMINATION
*** VIOLATION: Notice period is 2 weeks regardless of length of service. ***
[Note: This violates §622 BGB which requires 4 weeks minimum and longer periods for senior employees]

Termination for cause (außerordentliche Kündigung) may be immediate with valid reason.

§9 NON-COMPETE CLAUSE
For 24 months following termination, Employee shall not work for any competitor in the technology industry worldwide.
*** VIOLATION: No compensation specified for non-compete period. ***
[Note: German law requires at least 50% salary compensation during non-compete period]

§10 CONFIDENTIALITY
Employee agrees to maintain strict confidentiality of all business information during and after employment.

§11 INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY
All inventions and work product created during employment belong to Employer per German Employee Invention Act.

§12 DATA PROTECTION
Employee consents to necessary processing of personal data for employment purposes in accordance with GDPR and BDSG.

§13 WORKS COUNCIL
Employee acknowledges the right to participate in works council elections and activities per Betriebsverfassungsgesetz.

§14 APPLICABLE LAW
This contract is governed by German law. Employment disputes shall be resolved before German Labor Courts (Arbeitsgerichte).

§15 SEVERABILITY
If any provision is found invalid, remaining provisions continue in full force.
